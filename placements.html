Career Guidance Cell

Our Career Guidance Cell is dedicated to helping students unlock their full potential by offering expert career counseling, resume building workshops, aptitude training, and personalized mentorship sessions. It acts as a bridge between students and the professional world, ensuring they are fully equipped to face industry challenges and make informed career decisions. The cell consistently organizes sessions to improve communication skills, boost self-confidence, and prepare students for both on-campus and off-campus opportunities.

  • One-on-one mentoring sessions
  • Mock interviews and aptitude tests
  • Guidance from industry professionals
  • Resume and LinkedIn profile enhancement
  • Support for higher education and competitive exams
