Start Application

Eligibility Criteria

Undergraduate Programs

  • Completed higher secondary education (10+2)
  • Minimum 60% marks in relevant subjects
  • Valid entrance exam scores (if applicable)

Postgraduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field
  • Minimum 3.0 GPA or equivalent
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Statement of purpose

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Awarded to students with outstanding academic records. Covers up to 100% of tuition fees based on entrance exam scores and previous academic performance.

  • Presidential Scholarship: Full tuition waiver
  • Dean's Scholarship: 50% tuition waiver
  • Honors Scholarship: 25% tuition waiver
